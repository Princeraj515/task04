maharana pratap

Maharana Pratap was a brave, fearless king of Mewar, which is a place in Rajasthan, India.
He was born in 1540 in the royal family of Mewar. He is famous for his bravery, nationalism, and fixed commitment to protecting Mewar from the Mughal Empire.
